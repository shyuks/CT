Cataract's Community & Support

There are a number of online communities and support groups that you can turn to for information and more. Here are several that are worth checking out:

VisionAware

Website: http://www.visionaware.org/forum/cataracts-forum/1

Community Description: A forum for those coping with cataracts.

Background: The website is designed to help and inform people suffering from cataracts and delivers a lot more than just an online forum.

General Comments: A great overall site for those with cataracts – from the community to the articles.

What It Does Best: Serves up a great online community.

What It’s Known For: Being a one-stop shop for people with cataracts.

The Drawback: As with any online forums, do not act on any information without first consulting with an eye doctor or professional.

MDJunction

Website: http://www.mdjunction.com/cataracts

Community Description: A community of patients, family, and friends who are dealing with Cataracts, together.

Background: MDJunction is a great website that hosts many different online forums.

General Comments: It’s a great home and resource for cataract sufferers and those who love them.

What It Does Best: Provides a loving community forum.

What It’s Known For: Being a source of information and support.

The Drawback: Ensure any information you glean from this site – whether legal or medical – is checked out with a professional before you act on it.

Health Boards

Website: http://www.healthboards.com/boards/lasik-eye-surgery/934970-support-group.html

Community Description: A message board for all things Lasik.

Background: The HealthBoards website is host to many different types of health-related topics.

General Comments: It’s very sparse and outdated. Nothing to see here (no pun intended).

What It Does Best: Claims to be there for people who had problems with Lasik.

What It’s Known For: Its problem is that it’s not known for anything – hence the sparse membership.

The Drawback: There’s nothing here of value.

American Diabetes Association - Lasik

Website: https://community.diabetes.org/discuss/viewtopic/3/11384?post_id=121499

Community Description: An online forum on the American Diabetes website designed for diabetes sufferers who want Lasik.

Background: The ADA is a well-supported medical group.

General Comments: Best intentions but falls flat as there are hardly any users.

What It Does Best: Gives information about Lasik surgery to people with diabetes.

What It’s Known For: A place to ask questions about Lasik.

The Drawback: It’s very sparse.

WebMD

Website: http://forums.webmd.com/3/eye-health-community/forum/1235/1

Community Description: A forum for people suffering from vision issues including cataracts.

Background: WebMD is a well-known website, and this is one of its many forums.

General Comments: There’s not much going on here; posts are over a year old, and there’s nothing we can see in the way of an active, friendly community.

What It Does Best: It trades off the WebMD name.

What It’s Known For: Being a part of WebMD’s online world.

The Drawback: Unfortunately, not too many people post here so it’s rather outdated.
