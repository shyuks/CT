There are a number of digital apps you can use that may help you with Cataracts and Lasik. Some popular apps are listed below.

>

Amsler Grid

WEBSITE: https://www.facebook.com/ossibus.apps

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/eyecare-amsler-grid-eye-test/id1016180237?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ossibussoftware.amsler

Function: Amsler Grid is an eye-test that can be used for identifying distortion in your line of vision.

Background: The Amsler Grid is produced by Ossibus Software who have other eye-related apps available.

General Comments: It can detect issues with your eyes early, and if you have macular degeneration then you can use this test daily to monitor it.

What It Does Best: Gives you fast check-ups at home or on the go – what you do with that information is then up to you.

Why It’s Famous: It’s fast and clean with no advertisements.

The Drawback: It doesn’t replace a full and comprehensive eye exam, so you’ll still need to see an eye doctor.

Be My Eyes

WEBSITE: http://bemyeyes.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/dk/app/be-my-eyes-helping-blind-see/id905177575?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY: N/A

Function: If your eyesight is worsening, use this app to talk to sighted people who can answer questions via a live video connection.

Background: It’s a free app (the Google version is in development) with over half a million users.

General Comments: Sighted users are also welcome to join this app to help those who are visually impaired.

What It Does Best: Connecting visually impaired people with sighted users.

Why It’s Famous: It’s the largest online community for the visually impaired/blind.

The Drawback: It’s still growing.

Lasik & Cataract

WEBSITE: https://www.focusmedica.com

ITUNES: N/A

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.focusmedica.ud.lasikandcataract&hl=en

Function: Provides video discussing the eye, refractory mistakes, and Lasik.

Background: Focus Media produce a series of apps designed at helping with diabetes and anatomy.

General Comments: The videos are good at explaining stuff, but you have to pay for additional videos.

What It Does Best: Giving you simple answers in their videos.

Why It’s Famous: Voted amongst the Top Apps in 2014.

The Drawback: In-app products sell for $7.49 per item.

CataractSurgery

WEBSITE: http://georgekongsoftwares.weebly.com

ITUNES: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/cataractsurgery/id482530875?mt=8

GOOGLE PLAY:

Function: A simulated game of cataract surgery to help with hand/eye coordination.

Background: The manufacturer – George Kong Softwares – makes a number of apps for the eyes.

General Comments: Aimed at eye doctors.

What It Does Best: It’s a great app for professionals.

Why It’s Famous: It helps an eye surgeon perfect capsulorhexis.

The Drawback: It’s not for patients.

Peek Acuity

WEBSITE: https://www.peekvision.org/peek-apps/

ITUNES: N/A

GOOGLE PLAY: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.peekvision.public.android&hl=en

Function: They make apps that come with multiple vision tests – so you have a better idea of what to expect before you visit an eye doctor.

Background: Peek Vision, the manufacturer, is made up of a group of experienced eye care pros.

General Comments: It’s easy to use and does the job.

What It Does Best: Tests your vision from many angles.

Why It’s Famous: It gets top reviews and has been installed thousands of times.

The Drawback: It doesn’t perform Lasik surgery itself – yet.
